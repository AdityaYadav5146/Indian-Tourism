Popular Destinations in India

Taj Mahal

One of the seven wonders of the world, the Taj Mahal is a symbol of love and an architectural marvel located in Agra.

Kerala Backwaters

Experience the tranquil beauty of Kerala's backwaters, nestled between lush greenery and serene waters.

Leh Ladakh

Situated in the Himalayas, Leh is known for its stunning landscapes, monasteries, and adventure sports.

Jaipur - Pink City

Known for its majestic palaces, forts, and vibrant markets, Jaipur is the capital of Rajasthan and a UNESCO World Heritage site.

Goa

Famous for its beautiful beaches, nightlife, and Portuguese heritage, Goa is one of India's top travel destinations.

Varanasi - Spiritual Hub

One of the oldest cities in the world, Varanasi is considered the spiritual capital of India, offering a unique cultural experience.

Rishikesh - Yoga Capital

Known for yoga and adventure sports, Rishikesh is nestled in the foothills of the Himalayas on the banks of the Ganges River.

Andaman & Nicobar Islands

Famous for pristine beaches, coral reefs, and rich marine life, the Andaman Islands offer a tropical escape.

Mysore - City of Palaces

Known for its royal palaces, gardens, and the grand Mysore Dasara festival, Mysore is a blend of history and culture.

Fatehpur Sikri

Fatehpur Sikri is a UNESCO World Heritage site near Agra, known for its grand Mughal architecture and historical significance.
